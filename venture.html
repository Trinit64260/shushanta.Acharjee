Business

Art For Change (2019- Present)

Co-founder

Nestled in Sylhet, Bangladesh, I co-founded the social enterprise Art For Change alongside Anupam Acharjee and Forhad Ahmed. More than just a business, Art For Change stands as a movement, giving a voice to the untapped talents of students from marginalized communities.

Our Mission is: "Enlightening marginalized communities through art."

However, we are more than a commerce. We deeply invest in young artists, providing essential resources and fostering their growth. In supporting Art For Change, you're not merely purchasing art; you're backing a vision—a world where every artist, regardless of their origins, is given the stage to shine.
