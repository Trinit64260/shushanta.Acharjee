About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate specializing in Entrepreneurship and Management at the Autonomous University of Barcelona and am actively exploring job opportunities.

My research primarily delves into the realm of social entrepreneurship. I investigate factors influencing social entrepreneurship, the legitimacy of social enterprises in developing contexts, their internationalization strategies, and the cultural barriers they encounter.

Professionally, I serve as a Lecturer at the ESIC Business and Marketing School in Barcelona and hold an adjunct position at Pompeu Fabra University. Before embarking on my doctoral journey, I earned a BSS in Sociology from Shahjalal University of Science and Technology and an MSc in Applied Research in Economics and Business.
